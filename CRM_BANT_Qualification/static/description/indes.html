CRM BANT Qualification

This module enhances Odoo CRM by adding a structured BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timing) qualification system.

Features

  • Adds a dedicated BANT field to CRM opportunities.
  • Helps sales teams assess prospects based on key criteria.
  • Improves lead qualification and prioritization.
  • Enables filtering and sorting opportunities by BANT score.
  • Fully integrated into the CRM interface.

Benefits

  • Identify and focus on high-potential leads.
  • Streamline the sales qualification process.
  • Make informed decisions to accelerate deal closures.

Available for Odoo 18 on all platforms (Odoo.sh, On-Premise).
